About

Who we are

We are team of computer engineers who wants to use their knowledge to save other's life. We want to apply current developed deep learning technologies to our medical sector and try to help them.

What we use.

  • Convolutional Neural Network
  • Transfer Learning
  • State of the art VGG-19 Imagenet model

We want to use deep learning for the analysis and diagnosis of biomedical and health care problems. The technique has recorded a number of achievements for unearthing meaningful features and accomplishing tasks that were hitherto difficult to solve by other methods and human experts. Currently, biological and medical devices, treatment, and applications are capable of generating large volumes of data in the form of images, sound, text, graphs and signals creating the concept of big data. Deep Learning is a type of machine learning algorithm that has deeper hidden layers of similar function cascaded into the network and has the capability to make meaning from medical sector.
